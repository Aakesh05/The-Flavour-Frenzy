Bites Range

Bites are small, bite-sized pieces of either Chicken, Broccoli or Cheese that are often seasoned or coated with various spices or breading, and typically served as a snack or appetizer. They are a popular and convenient option for those looking for a quick and tasty protein-rich snack.

Patties

Patties are small, flattened, circular-shaped portions of ground meat, vegetables, or beans that are often seasoned with spices and grilled, baked, or fried. They can be served as a main course or in sandwiches and are a versatile and flavorful option for both vegetarians and meat-lovers alike.

Toppas

Toppas are a crispy fried delicacy which is coated with egg wash and breadcrumbs sure to melt in your mouth with each bite
